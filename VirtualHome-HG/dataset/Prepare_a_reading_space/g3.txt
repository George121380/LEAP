Task name: Prepare a reading space
Goal: Find a book and put it on the coffe table in home office. Then open the window in the home office.
Guidance: Find the book on the bookshelf and put it on the table in the home office. Then open the window in the home office.
Logic: k1 then k2

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind home_office: item where:
            is_home_office(home_office)
        bind table: item where:
            is_table(table) and inside(table, home_office)
        symbol has_book=exists book:item: is_book(book) and on(book, table)
        if not has_book:
            bind book: item where:
                is_book(book)
            achieve on(book, table)

behavior k2():
    body:
        bind home_office: item where:
            is_home_office(home_office)
        bind window: item where:
            is_window(window) and inside(window, home_office)
        achieve open(window)
