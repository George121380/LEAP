Task name: Cook some food
Goal: Make some French fries.
Guidance: Find a potato and cut it into pieces. Then find a frying pan and put it on the stove. Turn on the stove and add some oil. Put the potato pieces in the frying pan.

Logic: k1
behavior k1():
    body:
        bind potato: item where:
            is_food_potato(potato)
        bind pan: item where:
            is_fryingpan(pan)
        bind stove: item where:
            is_stove(stove)
        bind oil: item where:
            is_oil(oil)
        achieve cut(potato)
        achieve on(pan,stove)
        achieve is_on(stove)
        achieve inside(oil, pan)
        achieve inside(potato, pan)
