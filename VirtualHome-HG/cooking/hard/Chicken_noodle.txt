Task name: Cook some food
Goal: Boil some noodles with chicken.
Guidance: Put the chicken on the cutting board and cut it into pieces. Find a pot and fill it with water. Put the pot on the stove and turn on the stove. Put the chicken and noodles in the pot.

Logic: k1
behavior k1():
    body:
        bind chicken: item where:
            is_food_chicken(chicken)
        bind noodles: item where:
            is_food_noodles(noodles)
        bind stove: item where:
            is_stove(stove)
        achieve cut(chicken)
        
        symbol pot_with_water=exists pot:item: is_pot(pot) and has_water(pot)
        if not pot_with_water:
            bind pot: item where:
                is_pot(pot)
            achieve has_water(pot)
            achieve on(pot,stove)
            achieve is_on(stove)
            achieve inside(chicken, pot)
            achieve inside(noodles, pot)
        else:
            bind pot: item where:
                is_pot(pot) and has_water(pot)
            achieve on(pot,stove)
            achieve is_on(stove)
            achieve inside(chicken, pot)
            achieve inside(noodles, pot)
