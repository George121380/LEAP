Task name: Prepare dinner
Goal: Fried a steak. Then cut it and put it on a plate.
Guidance: To fry a steak, find a fryingpan, put it on the stove. Turn on the stove and add some oil. Put the steak in the fryingpan. To cut the steak, put it on a cutting board. Then use a knife to slice it. After slicing, put it on a plate.
Logic: k1 then k2

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind fryingpan: item where:
            is_fryingpan(fryingpan)
        bind stove: item where:
            is_stove(stove)
        bind steak: item where:
            is_food_steak(steak)
        bind oil: item where:
            is_oil(oil)
        achieve on(fryingpan,stove)
        achieve is_on(stove)
        achieve inside(oil, fryingpan)
        achieve inside(steak, fryingpan)

behavior k2():
    body:
        bind steak: item where:
            is_food_steak(steak)
        bind plate: item where:
            is_plate(plate)
        achieve cut(steak)
        achieve on(steak,plate)
