Task name: Prepare dinner
Goal: Fried rice with egg.
Guidance: Boil some rice by pot. To boil rice, fill a pot with water, put the pot on the stove and turn on the stove. Then put the rice into the pot. After boil the rice, put a frying pan on the stove and turn on the stove. Then put rice, egg, oil in a frying pan. 
Logic: k1 then k2

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind rice: item where:
            is_food_rice(rice)
        bind stove: item where:
            is_stove(stove)
        symbol pot_with_water=exists pot:item: is_pot(pot) and has_water(pot) 
        if not pot_with_water:
            bind pot: item where:
                is_pot(pot)
            achieve has_water(pot)
            achieve inside(rice, pot)
            achieve on(pot, stove)
            achieve is_on(stove)
        else:
            bind pot: item where:
                is_pot(pot) and has_water(pot)
            achieve inside(rice, pot)
            achieve on(pot, stove)
            achieve is_on(stove)

behavior k2():
    body:
        bind egg: item where:
            is_food_egg(egg)
        bind pan: item where:
            is_fryingpan(pan)
        bind oil: item where:
            is_oil(oil)
        bind stove: item where:
            is_stove(stove)
        bind rice: item where:
            is_food_rice(rice)
        achieve on(pan, stove)
        achieve is_on(stove)
        achieve inside(egg, pan)
        achieve inside(oil, pan)
        achieve inside(rice, pan)
