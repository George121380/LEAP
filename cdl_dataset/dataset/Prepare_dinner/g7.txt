Task name: Prepare dinner
Goal: Cook a pot of fish soup.
Guidance: Clean the fish by washing it in a sink. Then cut the fish into pieces. Find a pot and fill it with water. Then put the pot on a stove and turn on the stove. Put the fish in the pot.
Logic: k1

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind fish: item where:
            is_food_fish(fish)
        bind stove: item where:
            is_stove(stove)
        achieve clean(fish)
        achieve cut(fish)
        symbol pot_with_water=exists pot:item: is_pot(pot) and has_water(pot)
        if not pot_with_water:
            bind pot: item where:
                is_pot(pot)
            achieve has_water(pot)
            achieve on(pot,stove)
            achieve is_on(stove)
            achieve inside(fish, pot)
        else:
            bind pot: item where:
                is_pot(pot) and has_water(pot)
            achieve on(pot,stove)
            achieve is_on(stove)
            achieve inside(fish, pot)
