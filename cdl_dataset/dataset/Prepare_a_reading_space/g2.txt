Task name: Prepare a reading space
Goal: Find the book and put it on the table in bedroom. Then turn on the light in the bedroom.

Logic: k1 then k2

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind bedroom: item where:
            is_bedroom(bedroom)
        bind table: item where:
            is_table(table) and inside(table, bedroom)
        bind book: item where:
            is_book(book)
        achieve on(book, table)

behavior k2():
    body:
        bind bedroom: item where:
            is_bedroom(bedroom)
        bind light: item where:
            is_light(light) and inside(light, bedroom)
        achieve is_on(light)
