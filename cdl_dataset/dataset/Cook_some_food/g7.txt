Task name: Cook some food
Goal: Get a bowl of ice cream and add some jam on the top.
Guidance: You can find a bowl and put the ice cream in it. Then put some jam on the top of the ice cream.

Logic: k1
behavior k1():
    body:
        bind ice_cream: item where:
            is_food_ice_cream(ice_cream)
        bind jam: item where:
            is_food_jam(jam)
        symbol bowl_with_ice_cream=exists bowl:item: is_bowl(bowl) and inside(ice_cream, bowl)
        if not bowl_with_ice_cream:
            bind bowl: item where:
                is_bowl(bowl)
            achieve inside(ice_cream, bowl)
            achieve on(jam,ice_cream)
        else:
            bind bowl: item where:
                is_bowl(bowl) and inside(ice_cream, bowl)
            achieve on(jam,ice_cream)
