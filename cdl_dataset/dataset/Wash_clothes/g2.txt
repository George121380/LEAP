Task name: Wash clothes
Goal: Wash all the clothes in the basket for clothes by adding soap to the washing machine and running it.
Guidance: The basket of clothes is next to the sink in the bathroom. Put all the clothes inside the basket for clothes into the washing machine. Put a soap into the washing machine. Turn on the washing machine.
Logic: k1

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind basket_for_clothes: item where:
            is_basket_for_clothes(basket_for_clothes)
        bind washing_machine: item where:
            is_washing_machine(washing_machine)
        foreach o: item:
            if is_clothes(o) and inside(o, basket_for_clothes):
                achieve inside(o, washing_machine)
        symbol has_soap_in_washing_machine=exists soap:item: inside(washing_machine, soap) and is_soap(soap)
        if not has_soap_in_washing_machine:
            bind soap: item where:
                is_soap(soap)
            achieve inside(soap, washing_machine)
        achieve is_on(washing_machine)
