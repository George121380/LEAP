Task name: Put groceries in Fridge
Goal: Put milk and egg on the table in dining room. Throw all the items in the fridge to the trashcan. Then put milk and eggs back in the fridge.
Guidance: Put milk and egg on the table in dining room. Throw all the items in the fridge to the trashcan. Then put milk and eggs back in the fridge.

Logic: k1 then k2 then k3

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind milk: item where:
            is_milk(milk)
        bind eggs: item where:
            is_food_egg(eggs)
        bind dining_room: item where:
            is_dining_room(dining_room)
        bind table: item where:
            is_table(table) and inside(table, dining_room)
        achieve on(milk, table)
        achieve on(eggs, table)

behavior k2():
    body:
        bind fridge: item where:
            is_fridge(fridge)
        bind trashcan: item where:
            is_trashcan(trashcan)
        foreach o: item:
            if inside(o, fridge):
                achieve inside(o, trashcan)

behavior k3():
    body:
        bind milk: item where:
            is_milk(milk)
        bind eggs: item where:
            is_food_egg(eggs)
        bind fridge: item where:
            is_fridge(fridge)
        achieve inside(milk, fridge)
        achieve inside(eggs, fridge)
