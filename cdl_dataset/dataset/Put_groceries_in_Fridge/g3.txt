Task name: Put groceries in Fridge
Goal: Throw all the items in the fridge to the trashcan.
Guidance: Open the fridge and throw all the items in the fridge to the trashcan.

Logic: k1

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind fridge: item where:
            is_fridge(fridge)
        bind trashcan: item where:
            is_trashcan(trashcan)
        foreach o: item:
            if inside(o, fridge):
                achieve inside(o, trashcan)
