Task name: Wash dishes with dishwasher
Goal: Wash the bowls and plates with dishwasher. And wash forks by hand.
Guidance: Put all the bowls and plates into the dishwasher. Turn on the dishwasher. Wash the forks by hand.
Logic: k1
S0_Actions: wash_executor(fork_2080), wash_executor(fork_2081)
S1_Actions: wash_executor(fork_2150), wash_executor(fork_2151)
S2_Actions: wash_executor(fork_2103), wash_executor(fork_2104)


behavior k1():
    body:
        bind dishwasher: item where:
            is_dishwasher(dishwasher)
        foreach o: item:
            if is_bowl(o) or is_plate(o) and dirty(o):
                achieve inside(o, dishwasher)
        achieve is_on(dishwasher)
