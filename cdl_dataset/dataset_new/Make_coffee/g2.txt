Task name: Make coffee
Goal: make a cup of coffee using the coffee maker, then add milk and sugar.
Guidance: Put a cup inside the coffee maker and turn it on. Then add milk and sugar into the cup.
Logic: k1

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind coffee_maker: item where:
            is_coffe_maker(coffee_maker)
        bind cup: item where:
            is_cup(cup)
        bind milk: item where:
            is_milk(milk)
        bind sugar: item where:
            is_food_sugar(sugar)
        achieve inside(cup, coffee_maker)
        achieve is_on(coffee_maker)
        achieve inside(milk, cup)
        achieve inside(sugar, cup)
