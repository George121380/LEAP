Task name: Wash clothes
Goal: Wash all the clothes in the basket for clothes by washing machine.
Guidance: The basket of clothes is next to the sink in the bathroom. Put all the clothes inside the basket for clothes into the washing machine. Turn on the washing machine.
Logic: k1

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind basket_for_clothes: item where:
            is_basket_for_clothes(basket_for_clothes)
        bind washing_machine: item where:
            is_washing_machine(washing_machine)
        foreach o: item:
            if is_clothes(o) and inside(o, basket_for_clothes):
                achieve inside(o, washing_machine)
        achieve is_on(washing_machine)
