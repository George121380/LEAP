Task name: Cook some food
Goal: Slice apple, kiwi, and banana. Then put them on a plate.
Guidance: Apple should be cleaned before slicing. Kiwi and banana is already peeled. To cut fruit, you should put it on a cutting board. Then use a knife to slice it. After slicing, put them on a plate.

Logic: k1
behavior k1():
    body:
        bind apple: item where:
            is_food_apple(apple)
        bind kiwi: item where:
            is_food_kiwi(kiwi)
        bind banana: item where:
            is_food_banana(banana)
        bind plate: item where:
            is_plate(plate)
        achieve clean(apple)
        achieve cut(apple)
        achieve cut(kiwi)
        achieve cut(banana)
        achieve on(apple,plate)
        achieve on(kiwi,plate)
        achieve on(banana,plate)
