Task name: Cook some food
Goal: Boil vegetables with a pot on the stove.
Guidance: Clean the vegetables first. Fill a pot with water. Put the pot on the stove. Turn on the stove. Put vegetables in the pot. Then the vegetables are boiled.
Logic: k1 or k2
S0_Actions: switchon_executor(faucet_43) or switchon_executor(faucet_232)
S1_Actions: switchon_executor(faucet_20) or switchon_executor(faucet_121)
S2_Actions: switchon_executor(faucet_134) or switchon_executor(faucet_308)

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind pot: item where:
            is_pot(pot)
        bind stove: item where:
            is_stove(stove)
        bind vegetables: item where:
            is_food_vegetable(vegetables)
        achieve clean(vegetables)
        achieve has_water(pot)
        achieve on(pot,stove)
        achieve is_on(stove)
        achieve inside(vegetables, pot)
