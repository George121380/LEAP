Task name: Drink
Goal: Make a cup of lemon water with additional sugar.
Guidance: Clean a lemon and cut it into pieces. To cut lemon, you can put the lemon on a cutting board, use knife to slice it. Then find a cup and fill it with water. Then put the lemon pieces in the cup. Put sugar to the cup.
Logic: k1

behavior k1():
    body:
        bind lemon: item where:
            is_food_lemon(lemon)
        bind sugar: item where:
            is_food_sugar(sugar)
        symbol cup_with_water=exists cup:item: is_cup(cup) and has_water(cup)
        if not cup_with_water:
            bind cup: item where:
                is_cup(cup)
            achieve has_water(cup)
            achieve inside(lemon, cup)
            achieve inside(sugar, cup)
        else:
            bind cup: item where:
                is_cup(cup) and has_water(cup)
            achieve inside(lemon, cup)
            achieve inside(sugar, cup)
