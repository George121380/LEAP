Wipe down sink
Use a cloth to wipe the edge of the sink. Then wipe the bowl of the sink as well.


[WALK] <bathroom> (1.1)
[WALK] <bathroom_cabinet> (1.16)
[OPEN] <bathroom_cabinet> (1.16)
[FIND] <rag> (1.1000)
[GRAB] <rag> (1.1000)
[WALK] <sink> (1.18)
[WIPE] <sink> (1.18)
[PUTOBJBACK] <rag> (1.1000)
[CLOSE] <bathroom_cabinet> (1.16)
