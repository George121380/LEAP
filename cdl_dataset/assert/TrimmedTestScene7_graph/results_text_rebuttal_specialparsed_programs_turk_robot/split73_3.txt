Clean mirror
Spray the mirror with a cleaner, take a dry rag and wipe the mirror down until its clean.


[WALK] <bathroom> (1.1)
[WALK] <cleaning_solution> (1.1000)
[FIND] <cleaning_solution> (1.1000)
[GRAB] <cleaning_solution> (1.1000)
[FIND] <rag> (1.1001)
[GRAB] <rag> (1.1001)
[POUR] <cleaning_solution> (1.1000) <rag> (1.1001)
[PUTOBJBACK] <cleaning_solution> (1.1000)
[WALK] <mirror> (1.1002)
[WIPE] <mirror> (1.1002)
[PUTOBJBACK] <rag> (1.1001)
